  • Looking for a dress?

    • Browse: Go through our collection, search for few dresses you love.
    • Contact: Click on the What'sapp icon below to book an appointment and to try the dresses on.
    • Fitting: Time to try those dresses on! Our showroom has stylist who will not only help you make the right choice, and a tailor who alters  the dress to best fit you.
    • Decide and pay: Once you decide on the dress you like, you pay a deposit (50% of the rent price) to reserve the dress for you for the required date. Or you make a full payment in case you are going to take the dress on the same day of your appointment. An Insurance fees (Also 50% of the rent price) needs to be paid on the pickup day (You get back when you return the dress on time and in perfect condition)
    • Return: Returns have to be after a maximum of four days from the date when you borrow the dress (for example, if you rent a dress on Thursday to wear on a Friday, the dress has to be back to PH latest by Monday). Failure to deliver the dress on time will result in delay fees that’ll be deducted from the insurance fee.

  • Want to put your dress for hire?

    • Contact: Use the What'sapp button below to contact us 
    • Dress info: Send us your dress pictures along with details (Brand, price you purchased it with, its size and the year of purchase)
    • Evaluation: Our stylist will review your dress, and in case your dress is accepted, we contact you to book an evaluation appointment where you come to the showroom with your dress dry cleaned to evaluate the condition of your dress and discuss the way forward.
    • Sign a contract: Because we want to deliver pleasant experiences with our clients, we ask you to sign a contract that would help us save all rights.
    • Payment: In Posh Hire, we don’t buy dresses. However, we manage all dresses from photoshoots, marketing & PR, appointments to laundry and alterations in return of a margin fees (%) that is deducted from the dress hiring and/or selling price.
    • Return: If you would like to have your dress back, you definitely can. Posh Hire’s dress cycle is 6 months; which means you can have your dress back after 6 months from the submission date.

Size & Fit

We have one piece of each dress that would fit multiple sizes. We can size,almost all dresses, up or down for a perfect fit.
